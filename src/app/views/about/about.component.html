About

What is tr3Designs?

tr3Designs is an ecommerce mobile application that sells additive manufacturing, (3D printed), products. Products range from the smallest of trinkets such as jewelry, to common necessities like ashtrays and cutlery.


Mission Statement

"Display good energy through artistry."


Private Policy

What information do you collect? The information collected for tr3Designs includes;

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Email
  • Card Number
  • Card Expiration Date
  • Card CVC Code

How do you use the information? The profile informaton, (first name, last name, email), is soley used as a way to identify you as a customer. Credit card information is used to allow purchases.

What information do you share? There is no infomration that is shared.
